What's keeping construction crews on the quad this year?

THE DAILY TAR HEEL
SEPTEMBER 2014

Members of the Design Desk at the Daily Tar Heel share a passion for creating exceptional graphic-driven stories. "What's keeping construction crews on the quad this year," is my favorite select work from my several years at the paper. It won 3rd place for Illustration / Graphic / Cartoon in the North Carolina College Media Association 2014 Statewide College Media contest.

What Was Published

This is the final draft of the graphic story as seen in the Daily Tar Heel. The story detailed informed readers of why the quad was such an eye sore during that semester: construction crews were removing and replacing asbestos-insulated steam tunnels under the campus.

Research Findings

An aerial view of the campus highlighting the location of the steam tunnels.

As UNC is a public institution, details from different departments such as the Facilities Services Department are considered public domain and available online. I was able to research all the necessary information and find visual references to properly explain the construction.

First Major Draft

Initially, only half a page was allocated for this story. Later on into the process, more space became available to reformat it and give the map more prominence.
