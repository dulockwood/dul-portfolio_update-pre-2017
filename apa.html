Amazon Prime Air Mock Advertisement

UNC SCHOOL OF MEDIA AND JOURNALISM
MAY 2014

In part thanks to the ambitions of Amazon, it may become a norm to see delivery drones flying across our skies. Viewing a prototype video of their future delivery service inspired me to feature the technology in a mock advertisement for a class final project. Since then, Amazon has moved forward with their developments, bringing science fiction closer and closer to reality.

Self Reflection

In retrospect, I can see a number of errors in the finished product. For example, the words on the screen move too fast to read at a comfortable speed. I noticed some quirks in the animation and a lack of synchronization to the audio.

In the future I would produce a voiceover, attempt to improve the fluidity of animations, and add more subtle detail to graphical elements. I now have a comprehensive understanding of the steps involved in creating a detailed, engaging, motion graphic.
