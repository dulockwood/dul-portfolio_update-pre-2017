The Logo

Immediately after the initial contact with the client, the creative juices started flowing. I didn't let inspiration go wasted, knowing I'd likely begin working under contract soon. A rebranding consists of many things, but a strong logo brings all the parts together.

Some thumbnail concepts deviated strongly from the direction of repurposing their original lighthouse logo. To give the NCSA its own character in comparison to the other state societies of anesthesiologists, I determined the best direction was to keep the lighthouse (iconic of NC) to fit with the society's slogan: "The Beacon of Patient Safety in North Carolina."
